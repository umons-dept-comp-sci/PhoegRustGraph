pub fn subgraphs<'a>(
    g1: &'a GraphNauty, 
    g2: &'a GraphNauty
) -> impl Iterator<Item = Vec<u64>> + 'a

Returns every occurence of the graph g2 in the graph g1 up to permutations among the orbits of g2.

The graph g2 must have at most the same order as g1.
